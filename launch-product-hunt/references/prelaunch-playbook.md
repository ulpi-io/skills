# Pre-Launch Playbook (the T-minus ramp)

Load at Step 4 for the countdown. The **outreach message copy** lives in the `launch-outreach` skill; this
reference is the **schedule, the audience-building strategy, the hunter call, and scheduling mechanics**.

## Build the supporter audience OFF-platform

PH **discontinued its native "Coming Soon" / teaser ("Notify Me") pages in 2025** (CTO: "a very old
surface area that was quite buggy," "no plans to revamp"). So there is **no PH-native pre-launch notify
list** — build the audience on channels you own:

- A simple **email-capture landing page** (Carrd / Beehiiv / ConvertKit / Mailchimp).
- **Build in public** on X/LinkedIn for 2–4 weeks.
- Be genuinely active in 3–5 communities you belong to **and on PH itself** — complete your maker profile
  and comment thoughtfully on other launches (real, established accounts carry far more weight than new
  ones). PH now points makers to **Product Forums** as a partial native pre-launch surface.

Target a warm list of **~100–300** before launch (Demand Curve cites ≥400 as a goal); roughly 40–60% of
launch-day engagement tends to come from your own audience. **Be honest** if the audience is thin — the
priority becomes building one, not blasting strangers.

## Self-hunt vs hunter (decide early)

**Self-hunt by default.** PH: *"We encourage makers to hunt their own products, and there's no discernible
advantage to using a third-party hunter."* PH reduced hunter weight in 2025; most launches are now
self-hunted. A hunter only helps in narrow cases (a first-time launcher with zero PH network). **Never pay
for a hunt** — *"Please do not pay anyone to hunt or send traffic to your product … will result in us
[removing] your product, and you could be permanently banned."*

## Schedule it

- The homepage runs a **24-hour Pacific cycle**; **schedule for 12:01am PT** for the full window
  (Submit → New Product → schedule; up to **1 month** ahead).
- **Tue–Thu** default (Wednesday most cited). Weekends get ~15% more "Visit" clicks but far less audience —
  a deliberate low-competition play only. Avoid Mon/Fri, holidays, big keynote days, and the early-January
  dead week. PH's own line: *"the best day to launch is the day on which you're most prepared."*

## The countdown

Scale to the real runway; if the launch is imminent, compress and say what's realistically still doable.

**Week −4 — foundation + seed audience**
- [ ] Lock positioning, tagline (≤60), and the narrative.
- [ ] Stand up the off-platform email-capture page.
- [ ] Start building in public (1–2 posts/week); complete your PH maker profile; comment on other launches.

**Week −3 — grow the list + relationships**
- [ ] Grow the email list toward 100–300 (demos, feedback asks).
- [ ] Engage in 3–5 communities you actually belong to.
- [ ] Line up 10–20 peers/founder friends who'll genuinely check it out.
- [ ] (Optional) embargoed heads-up to relevant journalists/newsletters.

**Week −2 — lock logistics + soft warm-up**
- [ ] **Schedule** the launch (Tue/Wed/Thu, 12:01am PT).
- [ ] Lock all assets (gallery 1270×760, thumb 240×240, optional 30–90s captioned demo).
- [ ] Soft "we launch in ~2 weeks" note to the waitlist; announce the date publicly.
- [ ] Draft the segmented outreach + the maker first comment (via `launch-copy` / `launch-outreach`).

**Week −1 — finalize + brief**
- [ ] Final asset QA inside the scheduled draft.
- [ ] Write the hour-by-hour launch-day runbook (`launchday-runbook.md`).
- [ ] Personally brief the team + top 20–50 supporters (heads-up only, no ask yet).
- [ ] Send the "we go live Tuesday — I'd love your honest feedback" email.
- [ ] Set up comment monitoring to reply within minutes.

**Launch eve**
- [ ] "Live tomorrow 12:01am PT" reminder + link; prep social posts; sleep.

**Launch day** → hand off to `launchday-runbook.md`.
